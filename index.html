Visit Utrecht

Enjoy sight-seeing and differents kinds of cafe's and restaurants in this vibrant city. You can also see or visit the iconic Dom Tower.

Top three activities to do at Utrecht

Attend the Jaarbeurs

is a building complex for large-scale events in the Dutch city of Utrecht. With an area of ​​100,000 square meters, the Jaarbeurs welcomes approximately 2.5 visitors each year.

Grab a cup of coffee or try eating out!

Discover your new favourite restaurant in Utrecht. A trendy cocktail bar, a cosy cafe, the best street food or a typical Dutch pub. Whether you fancy a quick bite or a full-on culinary experience, you can find it here.

Visit the shopping centres at Hoog Catharijne

If you are planning a day out shopping, Utrecht is the place to be. Stroll through its local markets, discover the best boutiques or visit Hoog Catharijne The Mall.
